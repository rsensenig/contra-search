About Me

Hi, I'm Rachel Sensenig, a full stack web developer living in the Boston-area who loves to build fun, accessible websites. After working in the environemntal non-profit sector for 10 years I discovered what I really loved was solving problems behind the scenes, so decided to pursue programming. I got into CodeSquad to learn HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Contra Search is my personal project from this program.

When I was 16 I attended a birthday party at a contra dance in Worcester, MA without even knowing what contra was - I've been hooked ever since! I created this website out of a desire to create an easy-to-navigate resource to find contra dances that also shows the energy and joy of the dance through its design.
